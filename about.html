About

What is Mahatm Innovations?

Mahatm Innovations is an applied AI lab focused on building practical systems for optimization, automation, and decision intelligence. The goal is simple: take complex workflows and make them reliable, explainable, and useful in day-to-day work.

What we build

  • LLM-driven assistants with clear safety boundaries
  • Optimization engines (constraints, scheduling, allocation)
  • Automation workflows that keep humans in control
  • Open demos and experiments in the lab

How we work

  • Build in public where it makes sense
  • Prefer systems and testing over flashy demos
  • Focus on privacy-first approaches when possible
  • Ship iteratively: small releases, frequent improvements
